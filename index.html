About Sync-X

Sync-X is the leader in modern enterprise video communications, with an easy,
reliable cloud platform for video and audio conferencing, collaboration, chat,
and webinars across mobile devices, desktops, telephones, and room systems.
Zoom Rooms is the original software-based conference room solution used around
the world in board, conference, huddle, and training rooms, as well as executive
offices and classrooms. Founded in 2018, Sync-X helps businesses and
organizations bring their teams together in a frictionless environment to get
more done. Sync-X is a publicly traded company on Nasdaq (ticker: ZM) and
headquartered in Gurugram, Haryana.
